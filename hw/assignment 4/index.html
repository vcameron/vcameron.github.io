How to Make Pasta

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Boil Water and Cook the Pasta

    1. Fill a large pot halfway with water and heat on a stove over high heat until water starts to boil--about ten minutes.
    2. Add a pinch of salt.
    3. Add a box of pasta to boiling water and cook for about 13 minutes, or until al dente, stirring occasionally

  2. Step 2: Make the Sauce

    1. In a large saucepan, add a large jar of pasta sauce of your choosing.
    2. Add 2 teaspoons of fresh minced garlic, a tablespoon of shredded fresh basil, and salt to taste.
    3. Add 3/4 cup of heavy cream.
    4. Let simmer over low heat for ten minutes.

  3. Step 3: Mix the Pasta with the Sauce and Serve

    1. When the pasta is cooked, drain the water and add it to the sauce.
    2. Garnish with fresh parmesan or mozzarella and more shredded basil.
    3. Serve and enjoy!
